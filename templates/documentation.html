Getting Started
Welcome to the Village Development Portal!

This portal is designed to provide you with comprehensive information about:

  • Government Schemes: Information about various welfare and development schemes available to villagers
  • Official Notices: Important announcements and updates from local administration
  • Local Services: Contact details and information about essential village services
  • Beneficiary Programs: Track beneficiaries and scheme allocations
First-Time Users
  1. Visit the Home page to see the portal overview
  2. Use the Navigation Menu at the top to access different sections
  3. Explore Schemes, Notices, and Services tabs
  4. For administrative access, use the Admin Login button in the top-right corner
Tip: You can access most features without logging in. Administrative functions require admin credentials.
Dashboard Overview
What You'll See on the Home Page
Statistics Cards

The home page displays key statistics:

  • Active Schemes: Total number of government schemes available
  • Beneficiaries: Total registered beneficiaries in the system
  • Notices: Current official notices and announcements
Search Bar

Use the search functionality to quickly find:

  • Specific government schemes
  • Official notices and announcements
  • Local services and contacts
Latest Updates Section

The dashboard shows the most recent notices and schemes. Scroll down to see all updates or visit the dedicated sections for complete details.

Viewing Government Schemes
How to Access Schemes
  1. Click on "Schemes" in the navigation menu
  2. You'll see a list of all available government schemes
  3. Each scheme card displays key information
Information Available for Each Scheme
Information Description
Scheme Title Name of the government scheme
Department Government department managing the scheme
Description Detailed information about the scheme objectives
Eligibility Who can apply for this scheme
Benefits What benefits are provided under the scheme
Application Link Where to apply (external link or portal)
Tips for Using Schemes Section
  • Read the Eligibility criteria carefully before applying
  • Note down the Department contact for any queries
  • Check the Benefits section to understand what you'll receive
  • Use the search function to find schemes relevant to your needs
Latest Notices & Announcements
Understanding Notices

Notices are official announcements from the local administration. They contain important information about:

  • Scheme updates and changes
  • Important dates and deadlines
  • Public holidays and office closures
  • New initiatives and programs
  • Document requirements and procedures
How to Access Notices
  1. Click on "Notices" in the navigation menu
  2. View notices in chronological order (newest first)
  3. Click on a notice to see full details
  4. Download attachments if available
Notice Information

Each notice includes:

  • Title: Subject of the announcement
  • Description: Full details of the notice
  • Publish Date: When the notice was published
  • Valid Until: Expiry date of the notice (if applicable)
  • Attachments: Supporting documents (PDFs, images, etc.)
Important: Always check the notices section regularly for updates that might affect you or your family.
Finding Local Services
What Are Local Services?

This section provides contact information and details about essential services available in the village, including:

  • Gram Panchayat Office
  • Government Schools
  • Health Centers & Hospitals
  • Veterinary Services
  • Agricultural Services
  • Water & Sanitation
  • Power Supply Office
  • Bank Branches
How to Access Services
  1. Click on "Services" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse services by category
  3. View contact information, address, and in-charge officer details
  4. Note down the contact numbers for future reference
Service Information Provided
  • Service Name: Official name of the service
  • Category: Type of service
  • Contact Number: Phone number to reach the service
  • Address: Physical location of the office
  • In-Charge Officer: Name of the person managing the service
  • City/Village: Location details
Beneficiary Information
Understanding Beneficiaries

Beneficiaries are individuals who receive benefits from government schemes. The portal maintains records of:

  • Registered beneficiaries and their details
  • Schemes they are enrolled in
  • Status of their applications
  • Benefits received
Accessing Beneficiary Records (Admin Only)

If you have admin access:

  1. Log in with your admin credentials
  2. Navigate to "Beneficiaries" section
  3. View, search, and manage beneficiary records
  4. Track enrollment and benefit distribution
Beneficiary Status Meanings
  • Pending: Application under review
  • Approved: Application approved, eligible for benefits
  • Active: Currently receiving benefits
  • Completed: Scheme completed or benefits exhausted
  • Rejected: Application rejected (check eligibility criteria)
Using the Search Feature
Quick Search

The search feature on the home page allows you to quickly find information across the entire portal.

How to Search
  1. Find the Search Bar on the home page or use the search icon
  2. Enter a keyword or phrase (e.g., "agriculture scheme", "health notice")
  3. Click the Search Button or press Enter
  4. Review the results from schemes, notices, and services
Search Tips
  • Use keywords: Search for "agriculture", "health", "education", etc.
  • Be specific: "Farmer pension scheme" gives better results than "scheme"
  • Try variations: Search for both English and local language terms if applicable
  • Use phrases: Search for complete phrases for more relevant results
  • Filter results: Review results from different categories
Pro Tip: If you don't find what you're looking for, try different keywords or browse the complete sections.
Account Management
User Accounts

The portal offers two types of access levels:

Public User

Access:
• View schemes
• Read notices
• Find services
• Search portal content

Administrator

Access:
• Manage all content
• Add/edit schemes
• Publish notices
• Manage beneficiaries

Admin Login
  1. Click "Admin Login" in the top-right corner
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Click "Sign In"
  4. Access the admin dashboard
After Logging Out

Click the "Logout" button in the top-right corner to securely exit your admin account.

Security: Never share your admin credentials. Always log out when using shared computers.
Administrator Guide
Admin Dashboard Overview

The admin dashboard provides tools to manage all portal content.

Key Administrative Functions

  • Add new government schemes with complete details
  • Edit existing scheme information
  • Delete outdated schemes
  • Manage application links and resources
  • Track scheme creation dates

  • Create and publish official notices
  • Set publication and expiry dates
  • Upload attachments and supporting documents
  • Edit notice details as needed
  • Archive or remove outdated notices

  • Add local services and government offices
  • Update contact information
  • Manage in-charge officer details
  • Organize services by category
  • Maintain accurate addresses and phone numbers

  • Register new beneficiaries
  • Update beneficiary information
  • Assign schemes to beneficiaries
  • Track beneficiary status (Pending, Approved, Active, etc.)
  • Monitor benefit distribution
Best Practices for Administrators
  • Keep information updated: Regularly review and update all content
  • Check dates: Ensure notice dates and scheme deadlines are current
  • Verify contacts: Confirm phone numbers and addresses are correct
  • Use clear titles: Make scheme and notice titles descriptive and searchable
  • Proofread content: Check for spelling and grammatical errors
  • Backup data: Regularly ensure data is properly saved
